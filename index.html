EAGLE
Eagles are large,powerful birds of prey belonging to the family Accipitridae, known for their keen eyesight,strong talons,and impressive wingspan.They are found across various regions of the world,including North America,Europe,Africa,and Asia.Eagles primarily feed on fish,small mammals,and other birds,using their sharp beaks and strong claws to hunt.
OWL
Owls are nocturnal birds of prey from the order Strigiformes,known for their distinctive round faces,large forward-facing eyes,and silent flight..
CROW
Crows are highly intelligent birds belonging to the Corvidae family,known for their adaptability,problem-solving abilities, and complex social behavior.
BUTTERFLY
A butterfly is a delicate insect known for its colorful wings and graceful flight. They go through a life cycle of metamorphosis,starting as caterpillars before transforming into beautiful butterflies.
GREEN PARROT
A green parrot is a vibrant bird known for its bright green feathers and ability to mimic sounds and speech.
PIGEON
Pigeons are adaptable birds found in urban and rural areas worldwide. Known for their homing ability, pigeons have been used for centuries to carry messages.
KINGFISHER
Kingfishers are brightly colored birds known for their excellent fishing skills. With sharp beaks and quick reflexes, they dive swiftly into water to catch fish and other aquatic prey
RED PARROT
A red parrot is a striking bird, known for its vibrant red plumage and strong, curved beak. These parrots are intelligent and social, often capable of mimicking human speech.Red parrots, like the Scarlet Macaw, are popular for their beauty and are often seen in tropical regions.
HERON
Herons are large wading birds commonly found near wetlands, rivers, and lakes. They have long legs, a slender neck, and a sharp beak, which they use to catch fish, frogs, and other small aquatic animals.
Ramphastos sulfuratus
The bird in the image is a Keel-billed Toucan (Ramphastos sulfuratus). This species is known for its large, colorful bill, which features shades of green, orange, red, and blue.
PEACOCK
Peacocks are stunning birds known for their vibrant, iridescent plumage and majestic displays. The term "peacock" typically refers to the male of the species, while females are called peahens, and together, they are referred to as peafowl.
